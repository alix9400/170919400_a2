About Proffessor Lovelace

Ada Lovelace is an English mathematician and writer who excelled in the field of mathematics at a very young age. She is well known for her work on the Analytical Engine. She was one of the first minds gifted enough to understand the full potential of a machine as much more than just an object used to carry out calculations. She has worked with many great minds such as Charles Babbage and her love for both mathematics and teaching is seen in every lecture.
